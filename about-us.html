About Us

Brand Story

T&C STUDIO, established in 2024, is a local fashion brand based in the Philippines, dedicated to discovering, designing and producing high-quality bags and hats. Inspired by the diverse culture of the Philippines, we focus on details, skillfully combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create unique pieces that blend cultural charm with practicality for fashion-loving Filipinos.

Our design team draws inspiration from global fashion elements while incorporating local cultural characteristics, creating product lines that are both international and distinctive. Each product undergoes careful material selection and strict quality control processes to ensure excellent quality.

T&C STUDIO is not just a brand, but a lifestyle. Through our designs, we are committed to conveying the beauty and vitality of modern culture, creating storytelling fashion experiences for both local and international consumers who pursue quality living.
